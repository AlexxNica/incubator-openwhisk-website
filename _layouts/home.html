OpenWhisk is a serverless, open-source cloud platform that executes functions in response to events at any scale, without managing servers.

Get Started

Where to start.

With OpenWhisk you can easily create actions, test, connect to other actions or debug them. Try the OpenWhisk CLI and run your first action in seconds.

Focus on your code. Only.

No need to manage servers, worry about scalability issues or resources fiddling around. Use your time doing what is important: Coding. Plus, you only pay for what you use and need.

Cost-effective

Pay only for the exact time your actions run, down to the millisecond. When an action is not working, it’s not in memory, so you don’t pay anything.

Languages & integrations

Work with what you know and love. Integrate services, code in the languages you feel more comfortable with, even custom ones. All the pieces connect.
